About

Hi, I'm Job Kelwon

"I love photography and shoot a wide range of personal and commission work."

Living in Kabarnet for the past 10 years, I've been able to work with many people across Baringo County. The majority of my work is self-initiated, focused on capturing images of individuals — both in portraiture and in motion.

I find the most interesting shoot locations in public areas like Weddings, Koitos, and general events. My commission work primarily consists of studio photography where I work to capture metaphors using props and styling.

I am the founder of Elite Media Creation, a team focused on capturing images of people in our city who embody the essence of our culture. We shoot portraits of families who have lived in the city for over four generations. If your family would like to be included in an upcoming photoshoot, please contact us.
